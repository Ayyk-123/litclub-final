ABOUT US

The Literary Club is one of the oldest clubs of IIT Indore. Since its inception in 2010, till date, it is one of the most active clubs of IIT Indore. The Literary Club of IIT Indore has always strived to develop an environment where literary excellence is both fostered and developed. With an initial focus on establishing a tradition that purports to the burgeoning urge to express-to express yourself with whatever assistance an imperfect dialect provides‐‐ the club has finally recognised the futility of this arduous task. But, well, it’s still fun.

Over the years, the club has also expanded by venturing into avenues featuring rhetoric (Monthly Debates) and journalism (Newsletter). IIT-I’s monthly newsletter ‘IMPRINT’, started in October 2014, is the recognition of journalism as a literary praxis. From covering college events to global issues, IMPRINT aims to provide fresh perspectives and developments in every edition. Along with the annual magazine ‘INSCRIPTION’, these two publications often keep the club busy all year. Apart from these the monthly events include Ransense, Cook a Story, Pottermania, Cache of Lexis, Codex Inspicio, Spell Bee, G.O.T. Quiz, M.U.N. and many more!

WHY THE LITERARY CLUB?

The Literary Club being the oldest club on campus, has a lot to offer. Follow your passion for literature with the continuous set of exciting events round the year. Prepare yourself to embrace challenges from outside the campus as well. Don't let your ideas to get confined in that small piece of flesh, instead bring it out on a piece of paper. If reading any book releases endorphins, dopamine and seretonin into your nerves, then you have come to the right place. Explore the subtlety of any particular language. Experience every emotion of a poetry. Make friends with the comic characters. Solve psychedelic puzzles and quizzes. Come, live in this utopia with us.

CODEX INSPICIO

Codex Inspicio is a book review competition on the book Farhenheit 451 by Ray Bradury. There is no particular constraint on the pattern of writing the review except that the word limit should be 800. You can write up your views about the theme of the book, or about the characters in the book and their traits. The review is then judged on the basis in which the review is written and upon your understanding of the story. There are also prices for this event worth Rs.1500 for the winners.
×

CACHE OF LEXIS

A one-of-a-kind event which requires you to wipe the cobwebs of your brain and exercise your mental faculties like you haven't done since your JEE! This event has a large participation every year. The objective is to crack codes, decode puzzles and connect all the dots, to reach the ultimate answer. If you are someone who loves solving problems, have keen observation skills and who can deduce answers to the most misleading problems; this is the chance for you to showcase your abilities. Of course there are hints everywhere around, it's just how close you look.
×

COOK A STORY

In this event, you are provided with a set of three visuals from which you need to cook a short story up. But there are rules and limitations! Firstly, the story must be an original composition. Ahough it can belong to any genre but there must be a clear connection between the story and all the three visuals provided and the story must have a title. There is no word limit. There are also prizes for this event worth Rs.1500 for the winners.
×

RANSENSE

Ransense is a creative writing competition of IIT Indore. So drill into the creative side of your mind and pick up your quills to carve a brand new piece of writing from scratch. A beginning paragraph is given to you and you will have to finish the story or you can even write a poem.
×

WORD WARS

Word Wars is the debating competition of IIT Indore. This event is a great opportunity for anyone who wants to improve his/her debating skills and enhance his/her performance in public speaking. But hold on because there are certain rules to follow. For each debate, there shall be 2 teams of 3 speakers each and the topic of the debate is given on the spot. The team in support of the topic is called the 'Government' and that opposes the topic is called the 'Opposition'. Each speaker is given 4 mins to speak in the alternaing order. First speaker is from the 'Government', then from the 'Opposition' and so on. Short questions and interjections are allowed. At the end, the winner team is given a prize money worth Rs.1500.
×

GAME OF THRONES QUIZ

A quiz competition based on the popular T.V. series of the Game Of Thrones. Held after the gruelling season finalle of this T.V. series, it was indeed a most exciting and thrilling competition of the year. The students were required to register in teams of 3. And of course, winners got to take home prizes worth Rs.1.5K.
×

IIT INDORE'S MODEL UNITED NATIONS

In this creative academic simulation of the United Nations and its organs, participants take up the role of international diplomats & representatives of countries in various UN bodies. Its aim is to develop universally acceptable proposals to solve some of the pressing global issues. It brings together an amazing assortment of young minds from across India to engage in and gain first-hand experience of negotiating by playing the role of diplomats for two days. For registration purpose and more info feel free to visit our website mun.iiti.ac.in.
